Project overview

The real estate sales & rental website is a web-based platform designed to facilitate property transactions between buyers, sellers, renters, and landlords. The platform provides a user-friendly interface for listing, searching, and managing properties, ensuring a seamless experience for all users. .

Problem statement

Finding and managing real estate transactions can be time-consuming and inefficient due to the lack of a centralized platform. Many buyers and renters struggle to find reliable listings, while property owners face difficulties in reaching potential customers. This project aims to bridge this gap by providing an efficient and secure online marketplace for real estate transactions.

objectives

  • a user-friendly website for listing and searching properties.
  • Implement secure authentication and user roles (buyers, sellers, agents, and admins).
  • Enable advanced search filters for location, price, and property type.
  • Provide messaging and booking features for smooth communication
  • Integrate payment options for rental deposits or property purchases.

    • Scope of the project

  • Users can register and manage their profiles.
  • Property owners can add, edit, and delete listings.
  • Buyers and renters can search and inquire about properties.
  • The platform will be responsive and work on both desktop and mobile devices.
